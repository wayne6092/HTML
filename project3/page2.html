This is
A Page About My Cats

From about the age of two was when my mom started bringing cats to our home, she would say that she just wanted to help them get to good health, but after she gave the cat a name it became family. I don't remember the first cat, but I remember a lot of them.

Remembered Cats

Here is a list of names I remember and other information that I can come up with:

Fatso

A white haired cat, not really sure what kind of cat she was, but she was a good mom to her six kittens. and she was a very loving cat, who loved to sit in my lap.

Rockstar

A black, white, and gray haired cat, she was a very assertive mom, she was always on top of the kittens like it was her job to protect them all, and she was good at it, when we took the kittens outside, Rockstar was always there watching out for them, or playing with them.

Oreo

He had mostly black sleek fur all around, but his underbelly was completely white, which is why we called him oreo in the first place. Oreo is the father of all of the twelve kittens, he was there for the little ones when they were born, and he stayed with the moms for about a month before he started leaving the house every day again, like he used to.

Star

A bobcat tabby mix, who had camouflage patterned fur, and the color was black and brown. When we moved to Ohio, we lived by the woods and my mom would feed the stray cats who lived in the woods, and one day a big bob cat started walking up into our backyard connected to the forest. My mom saw the bobcat, and she said that it was holding a kitten by the skin of its neck, like mother cats do. Then my mom opened the door to the approaching cat, who then drops the kitten on the doorstep in front of my mom. It was the winter time, so I guess the mommy cat thought that her baby had a better chance of survival living with caring humans, than in a cold forest with little food.

Magic

An all black cat, and was the second cat that was dropped off by the bobcat mother before taking off into the forest. My mom said the mom cat never came back. My mom took both of the kittens in and gave them a new life. Both cats grew up to be wonderful house cats, who loved us and we loved to give them our affection.
