Rules To Participate and Categories

We here at 'A Beautiful Reality' we want to give users the best experience that we possibly can while allowing individuals to participate in the photo contest. There really aren't a lot of rules, but we the rules we do abide by are strict, and cannot be broken if you want to be able to participate.

Rules:

  1. Please keep your uploads free from sexual content, refrain from uploading agressive/violent/hateful or offensive content.
    This site is purely for educational purposes and we cannot support the types of content mentioned above.
  2. When submitting photos, you are allowed only two submissions per day.
    On our website we want to be able to see everyone's photographs and have enough time between reviewing them and uploading them to the site.
  3. When voting on photos, please only vote once per month.
    You can vote on one photo per category each month.
  4. Upload requirements.
    We can only accept JPG, PNG, and GIF file types. We can store up to 1MB per image. All images exceeding these requrements will be discarded without notice.

Categories

  1. Architecture.
    Architecture is all around you, it can be found in cities, suburbs, and even in the most desolate of places, like monestaries built on mountain tops. For 'Architecture' upload we will accept any skillful photographs of buildings, temples, and structures.
  2. Flowers
    Flowers cover our planet, they are a beautiful part of nature's ecosystem, and plays a crucial role in the survival of life on earth. We will accept any skillful photographs of flowers, they can be big, small, long, and short flowers.
  3. Brids
    If you look up into the sky during the day there is a good chance you'll spot a bird flying, or a bird perched up on a branch of a tree. Birds are magnificent creature that thrive in the skies. If you can capture an image of a bird it will be accepted on to our site. All bird photographs accepted.

Thank you so much for participating!

We are so happy that you could join us to see the wonderful photos posted in this online photo club. Here at 'A Beautiful Reality' we enjoy spreading beauty to others around the world, and it brings us joy to allow others to do the same! You can find our sitemap in the top primary navigation, and you will find my email at the bottom of the page.

I am looking forward to reviewing your uploads!
