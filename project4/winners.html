The Monthly Wall of Winners

This page is dedicated to the last months winners of the photograph challenge. Since the site was just created we will be using photos that came from a collection of beautiful photographs from a graphic design website called Canva.com. I use it for every school project. We are currently accepting submissions for next months competition. So after the month of July, 2022, we will be holding our first ever photo competition, isn't that exciting!

Remember, beauty is in the eye of the beholder, and here at 'A Beautiful Reality' we believe that anyone can find beauty in anything, anywhere, at any time. The following photos were chosen for each category based on eligibility, profoundness, and an emphasis on beauty. We really hope you enjoy the winners page and the rest of the site.

Don't forget to reach out to the creator on the contact us page, or you can email them directly by going down to the bottom of the page and clicking on the email link. Or you can click right here --> Email: williams534@macomb.edu.
