Photo Community

Welcome to the public chat room page, this is where you can get in communication with other people that love to take photographs, with people who like to look at photographs, and with people who like to edit photographs.

Feel free to chat away, you can pretty much talk about whatever you want, as long as you follow the chat rules and guideline. Go ahead and talk about your favorite photographs, communicate on possible website enhancements, or service upgrades. Here in our public chat we can answer questions on the photograph voting, submission and winners page. Or you can just use the chat room to share your favorite photographs. You could also share your favorite places to capture photographs, or see if anyone else has a hidden/secret location where they get they take their photographs, the possibilities are endless.

Public Chat Room

  1. Please keep your uploads/messages free from sexual content, refrain from uploading agressive/violent/hateful or offensive content.

    If you behave in the chat innapropriately your messages will be blocked and you will be banned without notice.
  4. Have Fun!
    Respect one another, get to know each other, and most importantly, have fun!
