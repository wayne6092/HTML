Cast Your Vote!

Welcome to the public voting page!
Here at 'A Beautiful Reality' we pride ourselves on providing our users with everything they need in order to share photos, communicate about them and choose their favorite amongst the rest. As far as we have come in developing websites this one, by far, is one of our favorites. Each month I plan on changing the categories based on user suggestions and recommendations to our customer support.

The voting page will only hold three different categories of photography each month, and their will be three photographs chosen by us, to be placed on the voting page under their corresponding categories. Thank you so much for joining us, and for being a part of this wonderful journey through websites and photography! Feel free to share this websites with your friends, family, coworkers, and others so that they can be a part of the community to! If you know someone that likes to take photographs, then send them our link!

Voting Rules

  1. Please only click one button per category.
    This will allow us to tally the votes properly, and give us the opportunity to choose a winner based off of community votes.
  2. Vote one time!
    We appreciate your time and efforts in the voting process, and we want everyone to have a fair chance at winning, so please, only vote once per month.
  3. Choose your favorite!
    Each photograph is beautiful and unique, have fun, and choose wisely.
